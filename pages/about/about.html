About Indiethon

Indiethon is a tri-annual charity speedrunning event showcasing any and all types of indie games! Through our events, we show off games that you may not have heard of before to help support various amazing charitable causes.

We got our start in August 2021 when two prominent members of the Cyberpunk 2077 speedrunning community, nicnacnic and thedmpanda, came together to make an idea a reality. Through lots of preparation and promotion, we hosted our first online event in January 2022, with 40 runners showcasing 36 unique games. Through our generous donors, we were able to raise $3,730 for Child's Play over the course of our 36 hour event.

Since our first event, we've rapidly expanded to meet the demand and high expectations of our runners and viewers. We have developed multiple in-house tools to assist in running our events, such as our online tech dashboard for our volunteers, a custom donation tracking system, and Discord bots to help us organize and execute our events. Currently, we run 3 online events per year, with plans to expand to in-person once we have a plan and the capital to pull off such an event.

Over the course of our existence, we've raised $9,000 for our various charity partners, and we see no point in stopping now! We want to continue to raise money and awareness to causes big and small, while also making a positive impact on the speedrunning and general gaming community as a whole.

We'd like to thank everyone who has helped Indiethon get to where it is today, from our hard-working organizers and staff, our many volunteers that donate their time to help run the event, our wonderful runners who showcase their skills, and finally, our amazing viewers for continuing to support our content. Thank you all for your support, and we hope we can continue to put on these events for an eternity.

The Team

Our team is composed of members from various speedrunning communities. Staff generously donate their time planning and executing our events alongside our organizers, to raise money for some great causes. We can't thank our staff enough, as without each and every one of them Indiethon wouldn't be where we are now.
