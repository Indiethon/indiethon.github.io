Sponsorships!

Have an indie game that you're trying to get out there? Are you a brand trying to raise awareness for your product? You've come to the right place! Indiethon is the perfect place to connect with fans of indie games and promote your product. We offer a variety of advertising options with low or no sponsorship fees. Please see the options that we offer along with some information about each below. If none of these sponsorship types works for your brand, please feel free to reach out with your idea, we're always open to new suggestions!

Sponsoring Our Main Events

Indiethon's charity streams are where the action is at! During selected weekends, we put on a large online speedrunning event showcasing any and all indie games to raise money for some awesome charities! We usually get an average of 150 concurrent viewers and thousands of impressions, this is the best way to reach a large audience while still connecting with the indie game community!

  • Be showcased front and center during our main events
  • Multiple advertisement formats available (see below)
  • A small fee/prize donations may be required

Indie Game Showcase Streams

New this year, Indiethon is starting to run small streams to provide entertainment and audience engagement between our main events. The advantage with this is that you get a dedicated stream for your game to show off to a new audience. Some examples of content that can be ran are speed showcases, new game speedrunning challenges, beat the record events, and more!

  • Have a dedicated stream to yourself to explain and advertise your game
  • Content shown must be speedrunning-related in some fashion
  • Sponsors are required to find willing participants for the stream

    • Frequently Asked Questions

    What advertising formats do you offer?

    We are currently able to show your logo on our layouts, have pre-written advertisements read live by hosts, and play video advertisements during intermission. Depending on the sponsor, we usually do all three of these things. If none of these formats fits your needs, please reach out and we can discuss what would work best for you.

    Why do you charge a small fee for the main events?

    We ask our sponsors to pay us a small fee in order to cover some of our operating costs. While Indiethon is small, we have costs associated with the running of our business and events. Historically, the majority of these expenses have been paid out-of-pocket by the organizers, but as we start to grow we've realized that it's not sustainable to run things this way and are actively trying to move away from that model. If are concerned/not able to pay this fee, please reach out and we can negotiate something that is favorable for both parties.

    Are there any special requirements for each sponsorship type?

    For the main events, if the sponsoring entity is a video game or similar, we ask that the video game that will be advertised is an indie title. Otherwise, any sponsoring entity that is not a video game (video game products/services or other non-video game entity) are allowed to sponsor all or a portion of our events.

    For our Indie Game Showcase Streams, the sponsoring entity must be a video game or similar that is considered an indie title AND the content shown must be related to speedrunning.

    I don't like to use the word "sponsor", can you use another term to describe our agreement?

    Absolutely! Some of our sponsors don't like to be referred to as "sponsors", and that's totally fine. In the past, we have publicly referred to some sponsors as "supporters" instead. Of course, if you have another term you'd like us to use instead, we'd be happy to do so.

    What happens if there's a content disagreement between me and Indiethon?

    While working together, we hope to never have a disagreement between ourselves and our sponsors. However, we understand that sometimes our needs don't align with their needs. In that case, we will work together to find a common middle ground that accomplishes the goals of both parties. But, please remember that our main goal is to promote indie game speedrunning content, as well as raising money for awesome charities during our main events, and we reserve the right to have final say on what is shown on our channel.

    If you're ready for the next steps or you have any questions, please reach out to nicnacnic, Main Organizer and President of Indiethon Inc. Contact info is available on our contact page.
