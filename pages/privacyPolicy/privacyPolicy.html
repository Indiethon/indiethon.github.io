Cookies

Cookies are files with small amounts of data that is commonly used an anonymous unique identifier. These are sent to your browser from the website that you visit and are stored on your computer’s hard drive.

Indiethon uses these “cookies” to track logged in users across its website. This only applies to users with access to the administrative dashboard. Indiethon does not knowingly use “cookies” to track members of the general public without an account.
