What Is Indiethon?

Indiethon is a tri-annual charity speedrunning event showcasing any and all types of indie games! Through our events, we show off games that you may not have heard of before to help support various amazing charitable causes.

Since Indiethon was founded in August 2021, we've showcased lots of talented runners, many unique games, and raised over $6,000 for various amazing charitable causes. Thank you for the support from our runners, volunteers and viewers to continue to put on this awesome event!

Make sure to join our Discord and follow us on our social media to keep up with all things Indiethon!
